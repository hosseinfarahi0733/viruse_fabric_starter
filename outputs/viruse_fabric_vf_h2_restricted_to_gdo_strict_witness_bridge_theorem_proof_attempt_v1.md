## Proof

By RTSW-T-VF-H2-001-R, there exists a restricted strict witness r in the finite coordinatewise restricted toy domain.

By the embedding assumption, E(r) is an element of the GDO domain D.

By injectivity, E preserves the identity of the witness image.

By order preservation, the restricted order relation associated with r transfers into the GDO order relation.

By strict-witness preservation, the strict witness relation for r is preserved under E.

By ledger-update and activation compatibility, the embedded witness E(r) corresponds to the same abstract ledger/update/activation path in the GDO setting.

By positive-domain alignment, the strict positive-domain relation witnessed in the restricted setting is interpretable as a strict positive-domain relation in the GDO setting.

Therefore E(r) is a GDO strict witness.

Thus RTGDO-T-VF-H2-001-R is proved under the explicit bridge assumptions.
