## Planned proof idea

1. Use RTSW-T-VF-H2-001-R to obtain a restricted strict witness r.
2. Apply embedding E to obtain E(r) in the GDO domain.
3. Use injectivity and order preservation to preserve the embedded witness identity and order relation.
4. Use strict-witness preservation to transfer restricted strictness to GDO strictness.
5. Use update and activation compatibility to ensure the embedded witness corresponds to the same ledger/activation path.
6. Use positive-domain alignment to interpret strictness in the GDO positive-domain structure.
7. Conclude that a GDO strict witness exists.
