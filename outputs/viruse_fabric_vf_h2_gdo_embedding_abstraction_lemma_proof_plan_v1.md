## Target lemma 1

GDO-L-VF-H2-003:
restricted-to-general embedding witness lemma

Planned statement:
If the finite coordinatewise restricted toy domain can be embedded into the generalized ordered domain by an injective order-preserving map E, then every restricted strict witness has a corresponding generalized witness under E.

Planned proof idea:
1. Define embedding E from restricted finite coordinatewise states into D.
2. Require E to preserve order.
3. Require E to preserve strict witness relation.
4. Transfer the restricted witness through E.

Main risk:
This lemma is not provable unless E is explicitly assumed to preserve strict witness structure.

## Target lemma 2

GDO-L-VF-H2-004:
order-compatible abstraction map lemma

Planned statement:
If abstraction map Q from generalized ordered-domain objects to restricted coordinatewise representatives is order-compatible, then restricted coordinatewise dominance can be used as a sound sufficient condition for generalized ordered dominance.

Planned proof idea:
1. Define abstraction map Q.
2. Require Q to be order-compatible.
3. Show that dominance in the restricted representation implies dominance in the generalized domain only under this compatibility condition.

Main risk:
The implication may need to be one-way only. A two-way equivalence would overclaim.

## Planned outcome

The proof attempt may yield one of three outcomes:

1. Both lemmas proved under explicit embedding/abstraction assumptions.
2. One or both lemmas remain assumptions.
3. The generalized theorem must remain conditional with unresolved structure-transfer assumptions.
